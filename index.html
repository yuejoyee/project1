Sichuan Spicy Chicken

by Day Day Cook

Sichuan Spicy Chicken is a dish that has everything going for it. It’s legendary. It’s dramatic. It’s mouthwatering (to me, anyway). And despite its deadly spicy look, it’s actually really good.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of chicken thigh
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 pieces of star anise
  • 10g Sichuan pepper corn
  • 4-5 dried red chili peppers, chopped
  • 20g green onions, chopped
  • 5g white sesame seeds

Cooking Direction

Cut the chicken into medium sized pieces

Cut the dried chili peper into small sized pieces

Cut the green onion into small sized pieces

Add chicken to quickly cook for 2 minutes

Remove when the chicken begins to brown on the edges

Add Sichuan pepper corn, dried red chili peppers, and green onions to saute until aromatic

Add chicken to saute

Season with salt, soy sauce, and sugar

Add white sesame seeds and mix well

Garnish with chopped green onion

Ready to serve
